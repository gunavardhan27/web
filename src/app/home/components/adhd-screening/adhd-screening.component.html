Best Reading & Learning Strategies for ADHD

📚 Best Reading Strategies

🔊 Whisper Reading (Subvocalization Control)

Research shows whispering words while reading improves focus and reduces distractions.

📌 Implementation: Add a "Whisper Mode" where kids can repeat words after a soft AI-generated voice.

🖼 Focus Framing

ADHD readers lose track between paragraphs.

📌 Solution: A "Reading Window" mode that highlights only a small portion of text at a time.

🎨 Color-Coded Syntax Reading

ADHD brains process color faster than plain text.

📌 Solution: AI highlights sentence structure (Subjects = Blue, Verbs = Red, Objects = Green).

🔗 Hyperlink Engagement

ADHD learners stay engaged with choice-driven navigation.

📌 Solution: Make reading interactive by allowing kids to choose different "paths" or story variations.

👐 Kinesthetic Reading

ADHD learners benefit from physical movement while reading.

📌 Implementation: Add an on-screen "tap-to-read" feature where words appear only when tapped.

🎓 Best Learning Methods

🎭 Dual-Task Learning

ADHD brains focus better when engaging two senses at once (e.g., movement + reading).

📌 Implementation: Suggest "Bounce & Read" (sit on an exercise ball while reading) or "Walk & Listen" (audiobooks while moving).

⏳ Time Boxing

ADHD kids struggle with open-ended study time.

📌 Solution: A built-in "Sprint Timer" (Short bursts: 10-min read → 2-min game → 10-min read).

📝 Interactive Summarization

Instead of writing summaries, let ADHD kids use drag & drop key points (visual synthesis method).
